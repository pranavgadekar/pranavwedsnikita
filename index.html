How we met


We met through a mutual friend back in 2011. We did not think of it much, as our friendship had only just begun. After graduation, just like many others, our friendship was only birthday wishes and facebook likes. In 2018, fate put us together in New York City and we reconnected. A friendship which had hardly begun seven years ago blossomed into fist bumps, laughter, support & affection.

We harmonized by accident and rest as they say.." Once in a lifetime, in middle of an ordinary life , Love gives us a fairytale"
